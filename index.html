The Event

Ceremony & Party
  • El Camborio, Granada, Spain
  • 13 September 2025, 6.30pm until Late/Early
  • Black Tie

The ceremony and party will both be held in the same location, "El Camborio" in central Granada. The ceremony will start at 7pm, so please arrive on time. After the ceremony there will be a cocktail reception with a variety of dishes on offer followed by a free bar with a band and DJ.

It should be a really fun evening and we hope that you can join us, but please let us know either way in the RSVP form below.

Please Note
  • We are asking people not to bring their children along (we've made a few exceptions for immediate family) as the party is going to start and end late and the venue is unfortunately not child friendly.

Getting There

Granada has an international airport, but direct flights from the UK are only from London (Gatwick). If you live elsewhere, your best bet is probably to fly to Malaga which is only an hour away by bus or train. If you need any help working out the best way to get to Granada, let us know!

If you don't know Granada well, we strongly advise against walking to the venue. Even if it looks close to your accommodation, the hill to the venue is very steep and the streets close-by are meandering and easy to get lost in. If any of the options below don't work for you or you need more info, let us know!

Where to Stay

The wedding will be held in central Granada, so any centrally located hotels or apartments should be relatively close to the venue (see "The Event" section above for an exact location). If you're looking for a hotel recommendation, please consider these - all come recommended and should be well located to catch the shuttle bus.

What to Wear

We would like you to wear something formal that you feel comfortable in, the following is simply to inform you what to expect.

The Spanish have quite specific etiquette when it comes to weddings. You can read plenty about this online, but to save you a job the rule for an evening wedding is generally:

  • Men - A dark suit.
  • Women - Ankle length dress or jumpsuit (not white), no big hats.
