The Event

Ceremony & Party
  • El Camborio, Granada, Spain
  • 13 September 2025, 7pm until Late/Early

The ceremony and party will be held in the same location, "El Camborio" in central Granada. The ceremony will start shortly after 7pm, so please arrive on time.

After the ceremony there will be a cocktail reception with a variety of dishes on offer followed by a free bar with a band and DJ.

Getting There

Bus

If you don't know Granada well, we strongly advise against walking. Even if it looks close to your accommodation, the hill to the venue is very steep and the streets close-by are meandering and easy to get lost in. We will be putting on a shuttle bus service from the city centre (see stops and times below), but taxi services are also available.
